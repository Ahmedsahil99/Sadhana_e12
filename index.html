Events

Legal Clash

In the competition, two teams will participate simultaneously, each having 9 + 1 minutes to present their case. Participants must thoroughly research legal principles relevant to the given topic or case. Before starting, each team must declare their roles, such as prosecutor, victim, lawyer, witness, plaintiff, or defense. Each team will be assigned a specific time slot for their case presentation, which will follow a structured format, including Opening Statement, Presentation of Arguments, Witness Examination, Counterarguments & Cross-Examination, and Closing Statements.

SQL SPARKS

SQL Sparks is a competitive event where participants test their SQL skills through real-world database challenges. The competition covers complex queries, data optimization, indexing, and efficient data management techniques. Teams tackle multiple stages, solving problems that require expertise in joins, subqueries, and performance tuning. Organized by Team Stranger Strings, SQL Sparks is a highly anticipated event that brings coding enthusiasts together to showcase their problem-solving abilities and database expertise.

Coding and Decoding

Big Bounty was an exciting coding and decoding event where participants solved intricate puzzles and challenges using logic and programming skills. The competition tested their ability to encrypt, decrypt, and analyze patterns under time constraints.
